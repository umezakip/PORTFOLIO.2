About Me

I’m an American born designer with Japanese and South American heritage. Fruitful of this, I am natively trilingual in Spanish, Portuguese, English. I enjoy traveling to other countries, all sports, and photography.

At SUNY University at Albany, I received a bachelors degree in Economics as a first generation university graduate.

 

I have a passion for growth and the determination to succeed. As a multimedia designer with over 3 years of experience in the design industry I have and continue to learn more about design every day. Over the past couple of years working as a Graphic Designer I have honed skills in software including Figma, Adobe: Photoshop; Illustrator; InDesign; Premier Pro; After Effects, Canva, and more. The journey of mastering design does not end there. Since then I have also acquired Front-End Development skills (HTML, CSS) in order to create a portfolio website and showcase my skills in a personalized method. This is a testament to my goal of continuous growth and proficiency.
